    Unrealistic Dating Story

    The premise of the game talks about the dating experience of a young
    individual and his pursuit of a girl. The selection of choices presented
    to the user allows them to choose their own stories. It follows the
    journey from the moment in which Dave, the character in which the user
    is controlling, first met the girl until the ending. In which Dave could
    end up getting to know her even more, or ending up not getting the chance
    to interact with her at all

    A series of various different if statements were used in order to layer
    different scenes and to connect them all together. A way in which to determine
    the validity of the user's inputs was through a series of while loops, that if their
    input did not follow the possible choices, that they will be prompted to
    enter a valid input instead(ie. Entering an invalid number input prompts user to
    enter valid letter input. This checking mechanism allows for overall smoothness
    of the program, in which it will minimize the risk of the program encountering
    an error due to the invalidity of the input. However, due to the time constrain
    that we weren't able to include of all the elements of our storyboard into it.
    Independenly, I was responsible for the half of the storyline

    It was a form of procedural abstraction because it allows for maximum
    clarity in terms of being able to follow the various different scenes required
    for this project, both during and post-development.

    A story that really stood out was A Lover's Quarrel. I
    liked it because of the clear storyline and also of the way it was
    presented in a manner that was very easy to follow. Another one
    that I like was The Advanture of Spider Ham, I liked it because of the light-hearted nature of
    it, and also because it was not dense of text like some other programs.

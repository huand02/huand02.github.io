    • The Last Run


    https://scratch.mit.edu/projects/258846629/

    The premise of The Last Run revolves around Hannah, a girl who is
    trying her best to run away from the evil principal looking for late students
    to tardy sweep. I implemented a scoring system based on time that she survives.
    The use of randomness can be seen in position of the door. The player loses
    once they come in contact with Wanda, or the evil principal.

    ____________________________________________________________________

    The game was inspired by the everyday school environemnt. Two successful
    in creation of the game was the movement and the visual aspect of it. Two obstacles
    were figuring out how to use the given variables and also how to figure out
    how their use will affect my game. I overcame them by spending a lot more time
    on it. If I had more time, I would implement different levels of difficulties
    to the game
